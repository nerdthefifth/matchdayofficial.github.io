Our Mission

"Success is not a matter of luck, it's a matter of preparation." - Sir Alex Ferguson

Through data-driven research and insights, it is our goal to make the "beautiful game" clear and understandable. We hope to increase both fans' and specialists' knowledge and enjoyment of the sport by making football analytics approachable and entertaining. We wish to offer our findings in the hopes that it will assist proffessionals improve either individually or collectively. We also cover the various approaches to analysis and how to play and win at football.

About Us

We are a team of indivisuals passionate about the game of Soccer. We really enjoy watching the game and playing it above anything else. We try to analyse football matches, players, teams, tactics and other factors affecting the game. We also teach football analytics in order to raise awarness about the game and the new field that has sprung up in recent times.

Words from our Founder

I started watching football matches since 2019 and in excess during 2022. I noticed that whenever a team makes a certain move I understand what the team is going to do next. I realized later that what I basically do is make predicitons based on movement of players or rather the position of players and their indivisual capabilities. I also tried to map out the tactics of the team and changes in its formation. It was on December of 2023 that I took football analytics seriously and started writing a book on it. This led to the formation of MatchDay.
